Our Story

At Empire Body Waxing, we empower people to live big and live well by engendering confidence and connecting communities.

Empire Body Waxing was founded in 2020 by Avery Brush and Jenna Whitey. Both women had been in the waxing industry for years, but they had a vision for a company that did more than just waxing. Avery and Jenna wanted to grow people and give back to their communities in Salt Lake City and St. George, Utah.
