2. How long does hair need to be? Should I trim?
Hair needs to be at least 1/4’’ in length (grain of rice). Longer is better for a first-time wax. DO NOT TRIM. There is no such thing as “too long” but there is such thing as “too short”.
3. How long does a wax last?
A first-timer can expect new growth by week 2. This is due to your current hair growth cycles. Normally, by your 3rd or 4th wax we have your cycles synced up and we are pulling all the hair at the same time. This results in being smooth for weeks and right as hair starts to grow, it’s time for your wax! 4 weeks is the sweet spot for 98% of clients.
4. I’m plus-size, can I get waxed?
Heck yes you can! Empire waxes Every Body! We don’t charge any more or wax any less based on your body type. Get in here!
5. What kind of wax do you use?
For our Brazilian Waxes we use a combination of hard and soft wax.
Hard wax tends to be a little more gentle on the skin so we use it on the labia and scrotum.
Our soft wax is great for even the most sensitive of skin types and that’s what we use on everything else from eyebrows to toes!
6. Do you wax men?
Empire offers waxing for Every Body. We have full body services (back, chest, legs, etc.) and we have services that are Vulva exclusive & Scrotum exclusive!
7. Is it painful?
We don’t like the word “painful.” Keep in mind, we are removing your hair. We like the term “spicy” because it’s quick and it’s worth it.
We use high-quality wax (imported from Australia) and we don’t see the same irritation levels as other waxes. We also have a proprietary technique/protocol that is unmatched.
8. Can I get waxed on my period?
Yes you can!
We do require the use of a tampon (available in the salon) or a cup.
